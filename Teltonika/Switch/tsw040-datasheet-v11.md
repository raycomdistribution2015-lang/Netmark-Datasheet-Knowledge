### DATASHEET - TSW040

**FEATURES**


**Ethernet**


**Ethernet** 8 x ETH ports, 10/100 Mbps, supports auto MDI/MDIX crossover


**IEEE 802.3 series standards** 802.3i, 802.3u, 802.3ab, 802.3x, 802.3az


**INDUSTRIAL PROTOCOLS**


**Profinet** Profinet Class A conformance (available with optional order code)


**POE OUT**


**PoE ports** Port 1 - 8


**PoE standards** 802.3af and 802.3at compliant PSE ports, over spare pairs - Mode B


**PoE Max Power per Port (at PSE)** 30 W


**Total PoE Power Budget (at PSE)** 240 W


**Performance Specifications**


**Bandwidth (Non-blocking)** 20 Gbps


**Packer buffer** 128 KB


**MAC address table size** 2K entries


**Jumbo frame support** 9216 bytes


**Power**


**Connector** 2-pin industrial DC power socket


**Input voltage range** 7 – 57 VDC, overvoltage protection: 60Vmax, reverse polarity protection: 80Vmax,

surge protection: min 64.4V/max 71.2V breakdown voltage, 93.6V maximum reverse

voltage @4.3A maximum reverse surge current, 1- Vin, 2-PGND


**PoE-out input voltage range** 44 – 57 VDC


**Power consumption** Idle: 1 W / Max: 2 W / PoE Max: 246 W


**Physical Interfaces**


**Ethernet** 8 x RJ45 ports, 10/100 Mbps


**Status LEDs** 1 x Power LED, 16 x ETH status LEDs


**Power** 1 x 2-pin industrial DC power socket



**Other**



1 x Grounding screw
